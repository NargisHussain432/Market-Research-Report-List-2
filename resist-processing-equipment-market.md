<p><p>Resist processing equipment covers three main types: manual, automatic, and semiautomatic systems. Manual equipment requires operators to perform all tasks, providing flexibility but potentially increasing variability and labor intensity. Automatic systems, on the other hand, are fully mechanized, ensuring high precision and consistency but with higher initial costs. Semiautomatic equipment strikes a balance, automating certain processes while still allowing for manual intervention, which can enhance workflow efficiency and reduce labor while maintaining some degree of operator control.</p></p>
