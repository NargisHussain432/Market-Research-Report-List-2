<p><p>The turbocompressor market comprises various types, including centrifugal and axial turbo compressors, as well as other specialized designs. Centrifugal turbo compressors utilize a rotating impeller to increase gas pressure, making them ideal for applications requiring high flow rates and moderate pressure boosts. Axial turbo compressors, on the other hand, leverage a series of rotating and stationary blades to compress gas in a streamlined flow, suitable for high-speed and high-capacity applications. Other types may include hybrid models or specialized compressors for niche markets.</p></p>
