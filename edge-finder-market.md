<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Photoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge finder market types include mechanical and photoelectric types, each serving distinct applications. Mechanical edge finders utilize physical contact to locate workpieces' edges, providing reliability in traditional machining settings. In contrast, photoelectric edge finders employ light beams and sensors for non-contact detection, offering precision and efficiency, particularly in automated environments. Both types enhance manufacturing processes by improving accuracy and reducing setup time, catering to various industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics, where precision is critical for productivity.</p></p>
