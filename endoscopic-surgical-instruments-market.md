<p><p>The endoscopic surgical instruments market encompasses various specialized tools used in minimally invasive procedures. Dissection tools aid in tissue separation, while retractors help maintain visibility by holding back tissues. Irrigation and suction tools manage fluids during surgery, ensuring a clear field. Hemostasis and cautery tools control bleeding and coagulate tissue, promoting precision. Endoscopic drills are utilized for bone and tissue removal in orthopedic and neurosurgical applications, enhancing surgical outcomes and patient recovery through reduced trauma and improved access.</p></p>
