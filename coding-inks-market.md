<p><strong>The Coding Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC Type</li><li>XLPE Type</li><li>THHN Type</li><li>Nylon Rubber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coding inks are categorized based on their material compatibility with various substrates. PVC Type inks are designed for printing on flexible PVC surfaces, offering durability and good adhesion. XLPE Type inks are optimized for cross-linked polyethylene, providing resistance to chemicals and UV light. THHN Type inks are suited for thermoplastic high-heat-resistant nylon applications, ensuring excellent performance in electrical wiring. Nylon Rubber Type inks cater to flexible rubber surfaces, delivering flexibility and adhesion while maintaining print quality on dynamic surfaces.</p></p>
