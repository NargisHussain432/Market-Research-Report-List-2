<p><p>The digital oscilloscope market comprises two main types: portable and benchtop oscilloscopes. Portable digital oscilloscopes are compact, battery-operated devices designed for field use, offering convenience and mobility for on-site measurements. They are ideal for engineers and technicians who need flexibility. In contrast, benchtop digital oscilloscopes are larger, stationary instruments with advanced features and enhanced performance, suited for laboratory environments. They provide greater processing power, larger displays, and extensive connectivity options, making them suitable for detailed analysis and complex applications.</p></p>
