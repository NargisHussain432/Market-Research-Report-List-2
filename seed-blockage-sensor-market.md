<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Sound Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Seed Blockage Sensor market features two primary types: Photoelectric Sensors and Sound Sensors. Photoelectric Sensors utilize light beams to detect seed flow, providing accurate blockage detection by sensing interruptions in light. In contrast, Sound Sensors rely on acoustic signals to monitor the operational sound levels of seed flow, identifying blockages through changes in sound patterns. Both types play crucial roles in precision agriculture by ensuring optimal seed distribution, enhancing crop yields, and reducing wastage during planting.</p></p>
