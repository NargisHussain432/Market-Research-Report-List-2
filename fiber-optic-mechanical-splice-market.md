<p><p>The fiber optic mechanical splice market includes several types of couplings designed to align and connect optical fibers with minimal loss. Flexible couplings allow for slight movement and alignment adjustments. Swivel couplings enable rotation, accommodating fiber misalignments. Capillary couplings use a hollow tube to align fibers precisely. V-groove couplings feature a groove for accurate fiber positioning. Other types may include specialized connectors or innovative splice designs catering to specific applications, enhancing transmission quality and reliability.</p></p>
