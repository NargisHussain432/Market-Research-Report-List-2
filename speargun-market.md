<p><strong>The Speargun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rubber (or band) Powered</li><li>Air Powered (Pneumatic)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speargun market is divided into two primary types: rubber-powered and air-powered (pneumatic) spearguns. Rubber-powered spearguns utilize elastic bands to propel the spear, offering simplicity, lightweight design, and ease of use, making them popular among recreational divers. In contrast, air-powered spearguns rely on pressurized air to launch the spear, providing greater power, speed, and accuracy. This makes pneumatic models suitable for experienced users targeting larger game fish. Each type caters to different skill levels and preferences in underwater hunting.</p></p>
