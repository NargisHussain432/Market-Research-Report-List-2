<p><p>Transmucosal drug delivery involves administering drugs through mucous membranes, offering rapid absorption into the bloodstream. The market is segmented into passive and active transdermal drug delivery systems. Passive transdermal delivery relies on drug diffusion through the skin without external assistance, using permeation enhancers to facilitate absorption. In contrast, active transdermal delivery utilizes devices or systems, such as microneedles or iontophoresis, to enhance drug penetration through mechanical or electrical means, enabling controlled and efficient drug release.</p></p>
