<p><p>The screen reader market is primarily divided into cloud-based and web-based types. Cloud-based screen readers leverage remote servers to process and deliver accessible content, offering scalability and reduced local resource demands. These solutions often require an internet connection. In contrast, web-based screen readers operate directly within web browsers, allowing users to access and interact with web content in real-time. Both types aim to enhance accessibility for visually impaired individuals, but differ in their infrastructure and usage contexts.</p></p>
