These cleaners are typically connected to the pool's return line, utilizing the water pressure to propel themselves across the pool surface, collecting debris such as leaves, dirt, and small particles in a built-in bag or filter. <p><strong>The Pressure-Side Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Booster Pump</li><li>Filter Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure-side pool cleaner market is primarily segmented into two types: booster pump cleaners and filter pump cleaners. Booster pump cleaners operate independently, requiring a separate booster pump to enhance water pressure, allowing for more effective debris collection. In contrast, filter pump cleaners utilize the existing pool filtration system for power, meaning they rely on the pool's built-in pump. Both types serve to maintain pool cleanliness, but their operational mechanisms and installation requirements differ significantly.</p></p>
