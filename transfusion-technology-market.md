Disposables refer to single-use items such as bags, tubing, and syringes that ensure hygiene and prevent contamination. Consumables encompass reagents and kits used for blood typing and compatibility testing, critical for ensuring patient safety. Together, these components facilitate efficient and safe blood transfusion practices in medical settings.</p></p>
