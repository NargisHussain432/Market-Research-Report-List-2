<p><p>The aerosol insecticides market consists of various formulations, primarily foam aerosols and powder aerosols. Foam aerosols, often used for targeted applications, expand and cling to surfaces, effectively delivering pesticides to hard-to-reach areas. They provide thorough coverage and can be ideal for treating nests and entry points. Powder aerosols, on the other hand, release insecticidal powders that settle on surfaces, creating a barrier against pests. Both types cater to different consumer needs, including convenience, application methods, and pest control effectiveness.</p></p>
