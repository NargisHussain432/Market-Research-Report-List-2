<p><p>The adhesive primer market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Silane adhesives promote bonding with moisture-sensitive substrates, enhancing durability. Titanate and zirconate primers provide excellent adhesion on challenging surfaces, often functioning under harsh conditions.
