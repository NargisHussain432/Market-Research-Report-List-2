<p><p>The Smart Window Materials market comprises various types designed to control light and heat. Electrochromic materials change color when voltage is applied, allowing for adjustable tinting. Photochromic materials react to light, darkening in sunlight and clearing indoors. Thermochromic materials alter their opacity based on temperature. Suspended Particle Devices (SPD) offer variable tinting using suspended particles that align when voltage is applied. Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) materials can switch between transparent and opaque states. Other technologies include nanomaterials and smart coatings.</p></p>
