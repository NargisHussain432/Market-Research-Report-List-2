<p><strong>The Solvent-based In-Mold Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Component In-Mold Coatings</li><li>Two Component In-Mold Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-based in-mold coatings market is primarily categorized into two types: one component and two component in-mold coatings. One component in-mold coatings are ready-to-use formulations that do not require mixing, simplifying application and reducing preparation time. In contrast, two component in-mold coatings involve blending a resin with a hardener before application, offering enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are designed for use during the manufacturing process to create a finish that adheres to molded surfaces effectively.</p></p>
