<p><p>Benchtop pH meters are categorized based on the number of channels they support. Single-channel pH meters are designed for measuring the pH of one sample at a time, ideal for focused applications. Dual-channel meters allow simultaneous measurement of two samples, increasing efficiency in laboratory settings. Multi-channel meters can measure several samples concurrently, making them suitable for high-throughput environments. Each type caters to specific laboratory needs, ranging from basic testing to complex, multi-sample analysis, enhancing workflow and productivity.</p></p>
