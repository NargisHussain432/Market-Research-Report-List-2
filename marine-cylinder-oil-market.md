<p><strong>The Marine Cylinder Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-BN (70-100 BN)</li><li>Low-BN (15-60 BN)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Cylinder Oil market is segmented into High-BN (70-100 BN) and Low-BN (15-60 BN) oils based on their Base Number (BN) values, which indicate their alkalinity. High-BN oils are designed for vessels using high-sulfur fuels, providing superior protection against acid buildup in the engine. In contrast, Low-BN oils cater to engines running on low-sulfur fuels, offering adequate protection while being less aggressive. This division helps shipowners select the appropriate lubricant for engine efficiency and compliance with emissions regulations.</p></p>
