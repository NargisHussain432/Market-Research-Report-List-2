<p><p>The Diagnostic and Interventional Cardiology Devices market encompasses various specialized devices. Transcatheter angiography devices visualize blood vessels, while intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) devices provide real-time imaging of vascular structures. Guide wire-based devices assess stenosis through fractional flow reserve (FFR) measurements, optimizing treatment strategies. Intracardiac echocardiography (ICE) devices offer detailed cardiac imaging from within the heart. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) delivers high-resolution images of vessels. Finally, robotic-assisted surgical devices enhance precision in cardiovascular interventions, improving patient outcomes and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
