<p><p>The foodservice gloves market consists of three main types: latex gloves, rubber gloves, and non-latex gloves (nitrile or vinyl). Latex gloves offer excellent flexibility and tactile sensitivity but may cause allergies in some users. Rubber gloves provide good protection and durability, commonly used in various cleaning tasks. Non-latex gloves, including nitrile and vinyl, are ideal for those with latex allergies, offering good chemical resistance and comfort, making them popular choices for food handling and preparation in the foodservice industry.</p></p>
